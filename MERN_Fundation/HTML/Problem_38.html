Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is one of the most famous travel destinations in the world. Known as the "City of Light", Paris is celebrated for its art, history, architecture, culture, and romance.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – the symbol of Paris and one of the most iconic monuments in the world.
  2. Louvre Museum – home to thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – the most famous avenue in Paris, perfect for shopping and sightseeing.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – located at Montmartre, offering panoramic views of the city.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – buttery, flaky pastries best enjoyed with coffee.
  • Crêpes – thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Macarons – colorful, delicate French cookies with creamy filling.
  • Escargots – a traditional French delicacy made with snails and garlic butter.
  • Ratatouille – a classic vegetable dish from the Provence region.
