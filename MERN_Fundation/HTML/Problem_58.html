Summary

*Inception* is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who enters the dreams of others to steal secrets. Cobb and his team are tasked with the seemingly impossible mission of planting an idea into someone’s subconscious, a process known as "inception."
